# Chronic Crime Behavior through the lens of Mental Health and Other Social Dimensions
## An examination of the 2016 Inmates Survey

The objective of this dynamic visualization is to inform the reader about the disparities in recidivism rates as we explore different social conditions than inmates have suffered/experienced, such as homelessness, foster family care experiences, emotional difficulties and mental health prevalence. 


See deployment here: https://jramtos.github.io/dynamic_vis/
