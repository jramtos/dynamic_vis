# Chronic Crime Behavior through the lens of Mental Health and Other Social Dimensions
## An examination of the 2016 Inmates Survey

The objective of this dynamic visualization is to inform the viewer about the disparities in recidivism rates as we explore different social conditions than inmates have suffered/experienced, such as homelessness, drug addiction, foster family care experiences and mental health. 


See deployment here: https://jramtos.github.io/dynamic_vis/
